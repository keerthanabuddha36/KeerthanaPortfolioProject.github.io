Airbnb Market Analysis – Tableau Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes Airbnb listing data to understand how property characteristics—especially number of bedrooms—impact pricing and availability. The goal was to uncover market patterns that can support data-driven decisions around pricing strategy, demand planning, and inventory optimization.

Using Tableau, I built an interactive dashboard that allows users to explore listing distribution, price variation, and availability trends across different bedroom categories.

What This Dashboard Shows

  • Distribution of Airbnb listings by number of bedrooms

  • Comparison of average prices across bedroom categories

  • Availability trends to identify high-demand vs less stable segments

  • Interactive filters to explore patterns dynamically

 

Key Insights

  • 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom listings make up the majority of the market, indicating stronger demand for smaller properties

  • Average prices increase steadily with higher bedroom counts, but price variability is significantly higher for larger properties

  • Listings with fewer bedrooms show more consistent availability, suggesting they are more reliable revenue drivers

  • Larger properties command premium pricing but may experience lower or more seasonal occupancy

Business Value

This analysis helps stakeholders:

  • Identify high-demand property segments

  • Evaluate pricing opportunities by bedroom category

  • Understand trade-offs between higher pricing and availability consistency

  • Support decisions related to property investment and inventory mix

Tools & Skills Used

  • Tableau – interactive dashboard design and visual analytics

  • Data Analysis – trend analysis, comparative analysis

  • Business Analytics – translating visual insights into actionable decisions
