Data Professional Survey Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes survey data from data professionals to understand career trends, compensation, job satisfaction, and skill distribution across the data industry. The goal was to transform raw survey responses into a clear, interactive dashboard that highlights patterns relevant to hiring, career growth, and workforce planning.

The dashboard was built in Power BI, with a focus on clean data modeling, meaningful KPIs, and intuitive visual storytelling.

What This Dashboard Shows

  • Distribution of respondents by job role (Data Analyst, Data Engineer, Data Scientist, etc.)

  • Salary trends across roles and experience levels

  • Preferred programming languages and tools

  • Job satisfaction metrics related to work-life balance and compensation

  • Demographic breakdowns for contextual analysis

Key Insights

  • Data Analysts and Data Scientists represent a large portion of survey respondents

  • Compensation varies significantly by role, with more specialized roles showing higher median salaries

  • Python and SQL emerge as the most commonly used tools across roles

  • Job satisfaction is influenced not just by salary, but also by work-life balance and role clarity

Business Value

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:

  • Understand compensation benchmarks across data roles

  • Identify in-demand skills and tools in the data job market

  • Support hiring, workforce planning, and career development decisions

  • Explore trends interactively through role- and skill-based filtering

Tools & Skills Used

  • Power BI – interactive dashboard design and storytelling

  • Power Query – data cleaning and transformation

  • Data Modeling – relationships and calculated fields

  • DAX – measures and aggregations for KPIs

  • Survey Data Analysis – trend and comparative analysis
